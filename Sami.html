Geneva

City Center

The heart of Geneva is full of charm — elegant streets, luxury shops, and the famous Jet d’Eau. You can walk by the lake or visit the old town with its cafés and monuments.

Geneva's Stadium

History

Geneva has a long history dating back to Roman times. In the 16th century, it became the center of the Protestant Reformation with Jean Calvin. Today, it is a global city known for diplomacy, finance, and peace.

Famous Places

  • St. Peter’s Cathedral
  • Jet d’Eau
  • Palais des Nations (United Nations)
  • Lake Geneva and the Old Town

Local Food

Don’t miss switzerland’s specialties: cheese fondue, raclette, chocolate, and lake perch fillets.
