The History of hurling.

Hurling has no single invention date but has existed in Ireland for over 3,000 years, with its origins rooted in mythology and ancient Hurling is one of the oldest field games in the world and is popular for at least 3000 years in Ireland with the first literary reference dating back to 1272. Skeheenarinky are a team in south Tipperary. They play in intermediate rankings and they also have a juniour b team. Some popular players from the club are James barry (former tipperary hurler) and lots of tipperary former and present players like Thomas Vaughan,Darragh Brennan and Connor Sweeney. Skeheen also have lots of important people of the pitch like a now official refferee Declan Hickey.
